Welcome to Code Skill Africa!


...Building African tech innovators.

''The future is written in code.''

At Code Skill Africa, we are igniting a tech revolution across the continent by empowering African youth with the skills to shape the future. Through our flagship programs in Web Development and Cyber Security, we are democratizing access to world-class tech education, offering scholarships and laptops to talented individuals who dare to dream big. With the generous support of our partners and sponsors, we are breaking down barriers and creating opportunities for thousands of young Africans to unlock their potential, drive innovation, and become the catalysts for economic growth and digital transformation in their communities. By investing in the limitless potential of Africa's youth, Code Skill Africa is not only bridging the digital skills gap but also fostering a new generation of tech leaders, entrepreneurs, and change-makers who will propel Africa into a prosperous and digitally empowered future.
